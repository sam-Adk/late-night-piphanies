"Each piece is born in the quiet of late night — graphite on archival paper, no digital editing."

Late Night Epiphanies is a collection of midnight-born pencil sketches and dreamy art by Samuel Adikah. Each piece is hand-drawn in the quiet of 3 AM, capturing raw emotions, surreal thoughts, and poetic stillness.

The late-night silence lets ideas flow freely, often turning a fleeting thought into a finished drawing before sunrise. What you see here is unfiltered — just a pencil, paper, and the strange clarity that only comes at 3 AM.

01
The Process
I work exclusively with 2B–8B graphite pencils on 300 gsm acid-free cotton paper. No digital editing — just the honest texture of lead on paper. Each piece is signed, dated, and sealed with archival fixative spray.
02
Limited Editions
Every artwork is produced in a maximum run of 10 signed prints. Once sold out, the piece is retired forever. Each print ships with a Certificate of Authenticity and hand-numbered signature.
03
Commissions
Want something uniquely yours? I accept custom portrait and concept commissions. Whether it's a portrait, a memory, or a surreal dream — I'll bring your vision to life in the same midnight style.

Materials & Craft

  • 100% hand-drawn with 2B–8B graphite pencils

  • Signed and dated on the back by the artist
  • Protected with archival fixative spray for longevity
