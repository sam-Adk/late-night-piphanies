About Late Night Epiphanies

Late Night Epiphanies is a collection of midnight‑born pencil sketches and dreamy art by Samuel Adikah. Each piece is hand‑drawn in the quiet of 3 AM, capturing raw emotions, surreal thoughts, and poetic stillness.

The Process

I work only with graphite pencils on high‑quality archival paper. No digital editing — just the honest texture of lead on paper. The late‑night silence lets ideas flow freely, often turning a fleeting thought into a finished drawing before sunrise.

  • 100% hand‑drawn with 2B–8B graphite pencils
  • Some ideas are scrapped from pinterest and i make it available for you
  • Drawn on 300 gsm acid‑free cotton paper
  • Signed and dated on the back
  • Protected with fixative spray for longevity

Limited Editions

Every artwork is produced in a maximum run of 10 signed prints. Once sold out, the piece is retired forever. This keeps each work rare and personal.

Each print includes:

  • Certificate of Authenticity
  • Hand‑signed & numbered by the artist
  • Archival pigment print on matte fine art paper
  • Free shipping in reinforced packaging
